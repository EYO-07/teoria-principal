? Trialetic Demiurges

:: 2. The purpose of a demiurge is degenerate and flatten the conscience. The conscience is tridimensional (Emotion,Intellect,Will), flattening is reducing the third-dimensional state to two or one.
:: 3. The demiurges of intellect attracts and trap the conscience to intellectual things, theories, knowledges, and making the conscience forget about the reality.
:: 4. The demiurge of emotion attracts and trap the conscience to the sensation domain, to hedonism, to pleasure.
:: 5. The demiurge of will trap and imprison the conscience to solve only problems of reality, and making the conscience forget about his spiritual and intellectual nature.
